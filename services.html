Interior Brilliance and Exterior Protection

Welcome to Kendall Paint Pros, your premier destination for top-quality painting services that elevate the aesthetics of your interiors and protect the exteriors of your property. Our skilled team combines expertise, premium materials, and a commitment to excellence to bring your vision to life.

Interior Painting

Revitalize your living spaces with our expert interior painting services. At Kendall Paint Pros, we prioritize precision and perfection, using industry-leading techniques and premium paints. From color consultations to the final strokes, our team ensures a seamless process that transforms your home.

Our Process

  • 1
    Consultation and Color Selection: We begin with a personalized consultation to understand your style and preferences. Our experts guide you through a wide array of colors, helping you choose the perfect palette for your space.
  • 2
    Surface Preparation: Before the first stroke, we ensure a smooth canvas. This includes repairing any imperfections, sanding, and priming the surfaces for optimal paint adhesion.
  • 3
    Premium Painting: Our experienced team employs industry-best practices, using premium paints and tools to guarantee a lasting and vibrant finish. We pay meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that every corner reflects your personality.
    >
  • 4
    Final Inspection: The job isn't complete until we've conducted a thorough inspection. We ensure that every inch meets our high standards and your expectations, leaving you with a beautifully painted interior.

Exterior Painting

Your home's exterior is its first impression, and at Kendall Paint Pros, we're here to make it a lasting one. Our exterior painting services are designed to not only enhance curb appeal but also provide protection against the elements. From residential homes to commercial properties, our team is equipped to handle projects of any scale.

Our Process

  • 1
    Evaluation and Estimate: We start with a comprehensive evaluation of your exterior, identifying any repairs or preparation needed. You'll receive a detailed estimate, transparently outlining the scope of work.
  • 2
    Surface Preparation: To ensure longevity, we address any peeling paint, cracks, or other surface issues. Proper preparation is key to achieving a durable and appealing exterior.
  • 3
    Weather-Resistant Coating: We use high-quality, weather-resistant paints to shield your home from the elements. Our team applies coatings with precision, ensuring a uniform and protective finish.
  • 4
    Cleanup and Inspection: Once the project is complete, we leave your property spotless. Our final inspection guarantees that the exterior not only looks fantastic but is also safeguarded against the wear and tear of time.
